About Garden's

Meet Gardenia — the heart behind Garden's Day Care.

Fourteen years of love, education, and a deep belief that every child deserves a safe, joyful place to grow.

Founder & Owner

Gardenia Díaz

NY State Certified Teacher · M.S. Early Childhood Education
"Throughout my career, I've prided myself on the connections I've made with the children in our care. I know how much anxiety comes with leaving your child with someone new — so I've made it my goal to create a place where parents can make that transition with ease."

Gardenia holds a Master of Science in Early Childhood Education and a New York State Certified Teacher Professional License for Birth to Second Grade. She's also CPR and First Aid Certified — and fingerprinted for both the Department of Education and the Department of Health.

She's been working with children for fourteen years as a licensed professional, and her journey started where many do — volunteering in a daycare as a teenager. What started as an instinct became a calling. Today, that calling lives in three New York classrooms where real families come and stay for years.
