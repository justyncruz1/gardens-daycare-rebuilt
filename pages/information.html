Programs & curriculum

Thoughtful routines, joyful discovery, room to be a kid.

Our Creative Curriculum is structured by age group — so every child gets what they need, exactly when they need it. Nothing templated. Nothing rushed.

Our mission

A family-oriented daycare where children become a little part of ours.

When parents leave their children at our doorstep, those children become our children — they are like family. We understand that choosing a daycare is one of the biggest decisions a family makes, and we're here to answer every question along the way.

The world moves fast. Our children deserve a nurturing place to fall back on — a safe haven where their confidence can blossom into something beautiful.

Our approach

Nurture-based. Family-oriented. Built for each child.

We work closely with every family to create a nurturing environment that reflects both our care philosophy and the values of each home — and we keep parents in the loop the whole way.

Structured routines

Stable, predictable schedules that give children the sense of security they thrive in.

Rich learning rooms

Thoughtfully designed spaces that invite exploration, creativity, and discovery at every age.

Emotional intelligence

We help children understand and express their feelings — one of the most important skills they'll ever have.

Family-first communication

Open communication between teachers and parents, every day — so you're never in the dark.

Our curriculum

We follow The Creative Curriculum — because kids learn best when learning feels like play.

Hands-on, child-centered, research-based. Our classrooms are environments where children can explore, experiment, and create in ways that are meaningful to them — while we fold in the cognitive, physical, social, and emotional foundations they need for what comes next.

6 – 24 Months

Infants

Secure attachment · sensory play · early communication

For our youngest learners, the foundation of learning begins with trust and security. We help babies connect with the world around them through rich sensory play and one-on-one nurturing.

  • One-on-one interactions to build relationships
  • Tactile play with water, sand & safe materials
  • Tummy time, crawling & reaching activities
  • Songs, stories & simple sign language
  • Music & movement for balance and coordination
24 Months – 3 Years

Toddlers

Curiosity · language growth · first friendships

Our curriculum encourages toddlers to make discoveries in a safe, supportive environment. This is the stage where language explodes, friendships begin, and self-regulation starts taking root.

  • Block play & construction with natural materials
  • Storytime, songs & finger-plays
  • Group play that builds sharing & cooperation
  • Outdoor activities: running, jumping, climbing
  • Guided conflict resolution & emotional awareness
3 – 5 Years

Pre-Schoolers

School readiness · thinking · emotional skill

Our pre-school curriculum prepares children for kindergarten by fostering creativity, problem-solving, and social cooperation — through project-based learning that meets them where their curiosity lives.

  • Early literacy, phonics & letter recognition
  • Math & science concepts in everyday activities
  • Nature projects, gardening & outdoor experiments
  • Arts, crafts & fine-motor skill development
  • Peer collaboration & classroom routines
Safety

Secure doors. CPR-certified caregivers. Fire drills. Real-world protection.

Our Manhattan location is in Washington Heights — part of a vibrant, family-filled neighborhood near the George Washington Bridge and major mass transit. There's a community-run playground one block away where Garden's parents have been meeting up for years.

Our facilities live inside buildings with heavy, secure doors and modern entry systems — visitors dial in, we verify, then they're in. Fire extinguishers throughout the center. Regular drills so everyone knows what to do. The whole staff is CPR-certified in pediatric and adult cardiac assistance, renewed every two years.

Safety isn't a feature. It's the floor.
